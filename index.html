Grapes

Red Grapes

Grapes are good sources of vitamin C and fiber. Red grapes also contain flavonoids, which help protect our heart, arteries and blood vessels.

Green Grapes

Thompson Seedless Grapes make up about half of the table grapes in the United States

Cotton Candy

Cotton Candy grapes were an accidental hybrid created in 2011 by horticulturalist David Cain.
